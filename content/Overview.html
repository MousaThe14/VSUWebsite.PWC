Petersburg Wellness Consortium

The Petersburg Wellness Consortium raises consciousness about health factors and outcomes by empowering community members, sharing capacity-building resources and creating partnerships that promote a healthy community. There focus groups include: Chronic Disease, Oral Health, Youth Health

It was launched in Fall 2012 by Dr.Maghboeba Mosavel

The organization, which is made up of a alliance of stakeholders and WE (academic-community partnership), uses collective resources to try to improve the overall health of Petersburg
